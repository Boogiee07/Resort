Visitor's Guide

Location: Just 2 hours from Manila via South Luzon Expressway (SLEX)
Best Time to Visit: Year-round, especially during cooler months (November - February)
What to Bring: Swimwear, towels, extra clothes, sunscreen, and toiletries
Tip: Book in advance during weekends and holidays for better rates
